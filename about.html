About Us

Let us make your grocery shopping easy

Equal foods was founded with a mission to simplify the grocery shopping process. Our founders, Mary and James Smith, used to be overwhelmed by the huge variety of prices and deals every grocery store. So they came up with an easy, efficient grocery store idea where every items is the same price. Now shoppers don't have to worry or think twice about where their money is going.
